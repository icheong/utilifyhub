Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Client-Side Execution Guarantee

At UtilifyGrid, we prioritize your absolute privacy. Every utility tool offered on our platform runs entirely inside your local web browser utilizing HTML5 Canvas, client-side JavaScript, and Web APIs. We do not transmit your input files, credentials, parameters, text entries, or database strings to any backend servers. Your calculations and conversions remain 100% private to you.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

Like most standard websites, we collect basic, non-personally-identifiable analytical metrics. This includes browser configurations, operating systems, landing referrals, visit durations, and timestamps. This metadata is collected to diagnose performance errors, track navigation paths, and optimize overall site usability.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Cookies

We monetize UtilifyGrid using Google AdSense to serve programmatic advertising. Google, as a third-party advertisement vendor, uses proprietary cookies to display tailored advertising banners based on your preceding visits to this site and other websites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its affiliates to serve ads to users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may choose to opt-out of personalized advertising by navigating directly to Google's Ad Settings portal at adssettings.google.com.
  • Alternatively, you can opt-out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Data Integrity & Security

Because none of our tools upload your confidential contents to remote databases, there is no centralized repository containing your text payloads or keys. Our deployment runs completely on secure Cloudflare edge networks utilizing industry-standard HTTPS transfer protocols.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon public upload. We encourage frequent reviews of this document to remain fully informed of standard updates.

6. Contacting Us

If you possess any clarifying inquiries regarding this privacy framework, cookie details, or standard web analytics, please consult our Contact Page to reach our maintenance team.
