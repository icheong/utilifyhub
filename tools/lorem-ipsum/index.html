What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is a scrambled excerpt from "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" by Cicero (45 BC). It has been used as placeholder text in the typesetting industry since the 1500s, popularised by Letraset sheets in the 1960s and desktop publishing software like Aldus PageMaker in the 1990s.

Why Use Placeholder Text?

When designing layouts, using real content too early causes reviewers to focus on the copy rather than the design structure, typography, and spacing. Placeholder text lets you evaluate visual hierarchy without distraction.
