Unit Conversion Reference

The world uses two major measurement systems: the Metric system (SI) used by most countries globally, and the Imperial/US Customary system used primarily in the United States. Engineers, scientists, and international travellers frequently need to cross-convert between them.

Temperature Conversions

Three scales are in common use: Celsius (°C) used internationally; Fahrenheit (°F) used in the US; and Kelvin (K) used in science. Formulas: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32; K = °C + 273.15.

Data Storage

Digital storage is measured in binary (powers of 2) or decimal (powers of 10). This tool uses the common decimal convention where 1 GB = 1,000 MB.
