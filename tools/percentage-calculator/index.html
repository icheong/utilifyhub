Understanding Percentage Calculations

Percentages play an essential role in everyday calculations, whether you are analyzing investment margins, calculating shopping discounts, balancing household budgets, or working out tax indices like GST/VAT.

1. How to calculate X% of Y

Result = (X / 100) * Y
Example: 15% of $80 = $12.00

2. What percent X is of Y

Result = (X / Y) * 100
Example: 18 out of 24 = 75%

3. Percentage Change

Result = ((To - From) / From) * 100
Example: $40 to $50 = +25%

4. Increase or decrease by X%

Increase: Y * (1 + X/100)   Decrease: Y * (1 - X/100)
